## Overview

Eärgon tomato juice is an easy-to-make, low-sodium tomato juice. An interesting variation adds bacon substitute for a smoky flavor and 10 g of protein at the cost of a little fat and sodium (source: Cookbook_Tomato_Juice.pdf, Overview). This is a vegan, sauce/beverage recipe.

## Ingredients

* 1 can (12 oz / 340 g) unsalted, canned tomato paste
* 30 fl oz (3 cans / 890 ml) water
* 3 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
* 3 teaspoon black pepper

## Equipment

* 48-fluid-ounce (1.4 litres) pitcher or similar container

## Procedure

1. Add tomato paste, water, vinegar, and black pepper to the container.
2. Shake or stir vigorously.
3. Serve, or store several days in a suitable container in the refrigerator.

## Notes, Tips, and Variations

* Contents settle over time; shake vigorously before serving (source: Cookbook_Tomato_Juice.pdf, Notes).
* Easy to customize: add cilantro, garlic, dill weed, peppers, or a few ounces of spinach.
* Add 3 tbsp (2 servings) of bacon substitute for a smoky taste and 10 g of protein, at the cost of a few grams of fat and 0.2 g of salt.
