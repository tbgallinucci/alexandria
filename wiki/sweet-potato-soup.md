## Overview

This sweet potato soup is inspired by flavors of Southeast Africa and Asia, with the addition of ginger and lime (source: Cookbook_Sweet_Potato_Soup.pdf, Overview). Total time: about 1 hour.

## Ingredients

* 6 sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
* 1 tablespoon fresh grated ginger
* 2 cups chicken and vegetable stock
* 1 cup water
* 1 milk cream *(units unspecified in source)*
* 1 lime, juiced

## Equipment

* Knife
* Blender
* Stovetop
* Saucepan

## Procedure

1. Place the peeled potatoes, ginger, stock, and water in a large saucepan.
2. Bring to a boil, then simmer until the potatoes are softened and tender (approximately 30 minutes).
3. Transfer the contents to a blender along with the lime juice and process until smooth. Work in batches if needed, or use an immersion blender.
4. Return the soup to the pot, stir in the milk, and cook a few minutes until heated through.

## Notes, Tips, and Variations

* Adjust the consistency by adding more or less water, milk, and stock. Less liquid produces a thicker soup (source: Cookbook_Sweet_Potato_Soup.pdf, Notes).
